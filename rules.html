Guessing Game!

Back to the game

Rules: There is one winning number between 1-100, you get five attempts to guess the number. After every guess you will be given an indication of how close you are to the correct number, hot or cold, and whether to guess higher or lower. If you would like a more accurate hint, you can press the hint button, this will give you three numbers, one of which is the winning number. You may only have one hint per game. If you would like to play again or start with a fresh game press the reset button.
